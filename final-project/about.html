Vanguard Games

Vanguard Games was created by a party of two, the primary goal was to create a space for those who wanted to play these Trading card games. It would also allow an area to host tournaments, and hopefully broaden what seems to be a niche hobby.
Some long term goals would bet to:


  • Bring in more games to broaden our target audience
  • Allow a place for those who need through after school programs
  • Host Tournments and give back to the community through prizes
  • Create funding to get kids started in Trading card games
  • Maintain an active and safe space in the community
